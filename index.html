    Sharia Bridge Finance Explained

    The aim of Sharia bridge finance is to allow you or your business to meet your short-term funding needs while remaining in accordance with the principles of Islamic finance principles (Sharia). A key principle followed is the avoidance of payment or receipt of interest (riba). Instead of charging interest, Sharia-compliant bridge financing structures often involve profit-sharing arrangements or rental agreements. The specific terms and structure of Sharia bridge finance will depend on what your individual needs are.
